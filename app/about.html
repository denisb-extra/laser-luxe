ABOUT

LaserLux R&D Ltd. have the capabilitities of Micro Laser Technology Production. We specialize in R&D and manufacturing of medical products and devices, prototyping small series of subcontractor services

LaserLux is dedicated to meet the highest requirements on finishing quality and lead-time, while being able to provide its customers competitive manufacturing solutions.
With capabilities based on years of extensive and proven experience, the firm offers the micro laser welding of various materials, mechanical design solutions, product development, cleaning processes, clean room assemblies, thermal treatments of Nitinol and more.
